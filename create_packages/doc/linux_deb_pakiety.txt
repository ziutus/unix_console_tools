
Standard or Optional?

Standard bedzie zainstalowany na kazdej workstacji :D
Optional trzeba zainstalowac samemu...


Układ katalogów, np:
/opt/ibm/
IBM has registered name on lanana (The Linux Assigned Names And Numbers Authority) 
http://www.lanana.org/lsbreg/providers/providers.txt

Ogólne zasady:

/opt/<package>
/etc/opt/<package>
/var/opt/<package>

/opt/<provider>/<package>
